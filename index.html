ABOUT US


EMEM idea was created out of concern for the environment, out of the certainty that we can contribute to the protection of our environment and our communities.


We are action-minded, solution-oriented entrepreneurs who are passionate about spreading the culture of sustainable clean mobility and putting it into practice.


EMEM will bring to society completely new solutions for urban mobility. Just as the pioneering companies did in the twentieth century when they manufactured the elevator, the car and other inventions and innovative solutions that changed the way of life. Therefore, EMEM promises you to work on improving your life through innovation in your daily transportation

OUR FOCUS


As a company specializing in mobility products, services and solutions, our focus is on providing innovative delivery solutions using small electric vehicles. Our advanced technology ensures efficient and sustainable delivery, with a proven track record of solving complex transportation problems such as pick up and delivery with time windows. Our commitment to sustainability is aligned with the future of the automotive and mobility industry, where micro-electric vehicles are becoming increasingly popular. By leveraging our expertise in mobility and electric vehicle technology, we aim to provide reliable and efficient delivery services that not only meet the needs of our customers, but also contribute to sustainable future.

Our motto is to spread the culture of sustainable green mobility. Therefore, we at EMEM press the starting button for the future of urban mobility in the region and aim to enable people to move by themselves in a clean and sustainable way, by being one of the leading providers in the region of small electric vehicle transportation and means of charging and the smart platforms that support them.

OUR PRODUCTS


Our e-scooters and e-bikes are designed to be eco-friendly and cost-effective, making them an excellent alternative to gas-powered vehicles. By using our micro electric mobility solutions, people can reduce their carbon footprint and contribute to improving air quality in urban areas. Moreover, our smart charging platforms make it easy and convenient for people to charge their vehicles at home, work, or on-the-go, reducing the reliance on non-renewable energy sources. By providing sustainable and convenient transportation options, our micro electric mobility solutions help people live healthier, more sustainable lifestyles.

When it comes to the delivery of goods or the transportation, the first and last mile can be the most challenging and inefficient parts of the process. That's where our company comes in with our innovative first-mile and last-mile clean mobility services. Our planned fleet of micro electric vehicles are perfect for navigating crowded urban areas and delivering goods with minimal environmental impact. Whether it's the first mile from a warehouse to a distribution center, or the last mile from a delivery hub to a customer's doorstep, our micro electric vehicles will provide a reliable and sustainable solution. And with our state-of-the-art tracking system, you'll always know where your goods are in the delivery process.
